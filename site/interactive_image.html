Browse

This is the Browse window. Here you can see what other clients on the same router(s) and group as you share. Only clients who share something will appear here. You can download anything by either double-clicking it, or right clicking and pressing "Download".

Message

This is the Message window. This is where you can send private messages (PMs) to other clients on the same router(s) and group as you. Note that only you and the other client can see these messages, and are encrypted equally with all the other messages sent through demonsaw.
